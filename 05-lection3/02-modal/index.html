Protester critical and French police hurt in ‘horrifying violence’

A demonstrator is fighting for his life after an environmental protest ended in “scenes of horror” when police fought activists using petrol bombs, axes, metal spikes and other weapons.

The 30-year-old man suffered a head injury, adding to tensions over claims of police brutality during protests against

In Bordeaux, a couple were seen ignoring the chaos surrounding them, including a fire started by protesters, to enjoy their evening glasses of wine. The video went viral on social media where it was described as, among other things, “the most French thing ever”.
